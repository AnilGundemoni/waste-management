FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can service begin after signing up?

We can typically deploy bins and begin service within 3–5 business days for most commercial locations. Enterprise clients requiring site assessments may take 7–10 business days.

What bins sizes are available for hire?

We offer bins from 120L (small office) to 1100L (industrial) for regular collection, and skip bins from 2m³ to 30m³ for bulk/on-demand jobs. Our team will recommend the right size during your site assessment.

Do you provide recycling compliance certificates?

Yes. Business and Enterprise plan clients receive monthly certified diversion reports suitable for ISO audits, ESG reporting, and regulatory compliance submissions.

Can I manage multiple business sites from one account?

Absolutely. Our client portal supports multi-site management with individual site dashboards, consolidated billing, and a single analytics view across all locations.

How does the IoT bin monitoring work?

We fit ultrasonic sensors inside your bins that measure fill levels in real-time. Data is sent to our platform every 30 minutes. You receive automatic alerts when bins reach 80% capacity, and our system uses this data to optimize your pickup schedule.
