Our Mission

Turning Waste Into
a Resource, Not a Problem

Founded in 2011, EcoTrack began with a simple belief: that businesses should never have to choose between being environmentally responsible and running profitably.

Today we serve over 2,500 commercial clients, diverting 50,000+ tonnes of waste from landfill every year through smart technology, dedicated service, and a deep commitment to circularity.

Our mission is to make best-practice waste management the norm, not the exception — delivering measurable environmental and financial benefits to every client we serve.

Our Journey

15 Years of Growth & Impact

2011 – Founded

EcoTrack Established

Daniel Green and two colleagues launched EcoTrack with 12 commercial clients and a commitment to zero-landfill operations.

2014 – Expanded

Regional Expansion

Expanded operations to 5 cities. Launched our commercial recycling program, achieving 60% diversion rate for clients.

2017 – Technology

Smart Platform Launch

Released our IoT bin monitoring system and the EcoTrack client portal — a first in the Australian waste industry.

2020 – Certified

ISO 14001 Certification

Achieved ISO 14001 environmental management certification. Surpassed 1,000 commercial clients and 20,000 tonnes recycled annually.

2024 – AI-Powered

AI Route Optimization

Launched AI-powered pickup routing, reducing fleet emissions by 30%. Achieved carbon-neutral operations.

2026 – Today

2,500+ Clients & Growing

Serving 2,500+ commercial clients across 18 regions. Launching our next-generation analytics dashboard and mobile app.
