Real-Time Bin Monitoring

IoT sensors track fill levels across all your bins 24/7. Get instant alerts before overflow and optimize pickup routes automatically.

AI-powered routing reduces fuel consumption by up to 30%. Our system adapts in real-time to traffic, fill levels, and priorities.
